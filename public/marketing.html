💡 Promotion Strategy for Your Store
📱 Text Your Regulars

Use the At-Risk list — call or text customers who haven't visited in 30+ days with a personal deal. A simple "Hey Maria, 10% off this weekend — we miss you!" works great for small stores.

🎂 Birthday Specials

Check Birthdays This Week daily. Text members a birthday discount — "Happy Birthday! Enjoy 15% off your next visit this week." Costs nothing, builds massive loyalty.

📦 Post Slow Items Daily

Use the Slow-Moving Items list above. Post 2–3 items on FB Marketplace and Craigslist every morning. Takes 5 minutes and clears stuck inventory fast.

🖨️ Weekly Flyer

Click "Print Weekly Flyer" above — it auto-picks your slow movers and generates a printable flyer. Post it on your door, hand it to neighbors, or photograph it for social media.

🥇 Push Tier Upgrades

When a member is close to a tier, tell them at checkout: "You're only 50 points from Gold — that's 10% off every purchase!" The app shows their points automatically.

⭐ Google Reviews

After every transaction, tell happy customers: "A quick Google review would mean the world to us!" Even 10 good reviews dramatically increases foot traffic from search.
